Ravelry is a website for fibre artists to track their projects, browse, and sell knitting or crochet patterns. Ravelry has a ‚Äúhot right now‚Äù section which features the patterns with the most visits in the last 24 hours.

I used the ravelRy R package by Kaylin Pavlik to easily access the Ravelry api.
